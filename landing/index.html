What we're building
We run a heterogeneous compute fleet (NVIDIA DGX Spark systems, high-end workstations, Jetson Thor, and university clusters) to train, distill, and deploy vision-language models that actually control real robots in the wild — agricultural fields, greenhouses, and beyond.

Everything is orchestrated with Tailscale, custom SSH fleets, agentic workflows, and reproducible pipelines. The goal: scalable Physical AI.
